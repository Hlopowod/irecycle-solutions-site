Common Questions

Got a Question?

Contact us directly →
Is business collection really free? +
Yes — for all business and commercial collections anywhere in Warwickshire, there is no collection charge whatsoever. Household drop-offs at our Rugby centre are also completely free.
How do I know my data is actually destroyed? +
We issue a signed data destruction certificate for every storage device we process, listing the serial number, wiping standard used, and date of destruction. This is your legal proof of compliance.
Do I need to wipe devices before handing them over? +
No — in fact, please don't. Some software wiping can interfere with our certified processes. Just send or bring the devices as-is. We handle all data destruction to the correct standard.
Are you licensed to handle our waste legally? +
Yes. iRecycle Solutions Ltd is a fully licensed Environment Agency waste carrier and registered WEEE handler. Using us satisfies your legal duty of care under UK waste legislation — we supply documentation to prove it.
Can I get an ESG or sustainability report? +
Absolutely. Our asset reporting service provides a full itemised manifest including device type, serial number, disposal method, and total weight diverted from landfill — ready for your ESG or sustainability report.
Do you cover areas outside Rugby? +
Yes — we regularly collect from Coventry, Leamington Spa, Nuneaton, Warwick, Stratford-upon-Avon and surrounding areas. For locations further afield, get in touch and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
